Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 12 Feb 2026

SnapHome (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the SnapHome mobile application (the “App”).

This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decides to use our App.

By using SnapHome, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Images and Camera Access

SnapHome requests access to your device camera and gallery in order to:

  • Capture warranty bills
  • Store product purchase images
  • Attach images to items

All captured images are stored locally on your device in the app-specific storage directory.

We do not upload or transmit your images to any external servers.

b) Item Information

SnapHome stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Item name
  • Purchase date
  • Warranty duration
  • Category
  • Notes
  • Bill images

This data remains on your device unless you uninstall the app.

We do not collect this data on our servers.

c) Advertising Data (AdMob)

SnapHome uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Usage data

This information is handled by Google according to their Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not directly access or store advertising data.

d) In-App Purchases

SnapHome offers a one-time Premium upgrade using Google Play Billing.

When making a purchase:

  • Payment processing is handled securely by Google Play
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Google may collect transaction-related information

Learn more about Google Play Billing privacy:
https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Store your warranty items locally
  • Enable premium features
  • Display relevant advertisements

We do not sell user data.

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data (items, images, notes) is stored locally on the user's device.

We do not operate our own servers to store personal data.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

SnapHome uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (In-app purchases)

These services may collect information governed by their own privacy policies.

5. User Rights

Users may:

  • Delete items at any time within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

If you have concerns about your data, you may contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
